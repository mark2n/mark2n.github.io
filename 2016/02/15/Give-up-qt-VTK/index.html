Give up qt+VTK

2016-02-15

After three days of desperate “problem solving” process with QT+VTK, I decided to give it up.

The need for this environment was from once interview test. Considering I didn’t have much expereinces with C++ and QT, I prepared myself for a struggling setup process. But this is still much worse than I expected. Besides, this is not a must for me, so three days’s of struggling tells that I will not be enjoying with this way of working. So I gave it up.

People say you should work harder than where you feel comforatable. It’s true that sometimes the success is just a step away from where you gave up. But I have the philosophy that one should enjoy what he’s(she’s) doing, to have the best creativity and proficciency. So, this is it. A new page.
